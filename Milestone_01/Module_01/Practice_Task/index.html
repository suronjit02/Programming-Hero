A Journey to the Hills of Bandarban
Last winter, I traveled to the serene hills of Bandarban, and it was an experience I'll never forget. The fresh mountain air, the sound of waterfalls, and the breathtaking sunrise from Nilgiri filled my soul with peace. The simplicity of the tribal lifestyle and the warmth of the locals made the trip even more memorable. It wasn’t just a journey through nature — it was a journey within.



What I Bought
  1. A handmade bamboo hat from a local market
  2. Tribal jewelry from a roadside shop
  3. A packet of hill-grown green tea
  4. Colorful handmade scarves
  5. A wooden keyring shaped like a mountain
  6. Local snacks and sweets for friends and family
My Favorite Foods
  • Bamboo chicken (cooked inside bamboo sticks)
  • Hill-style beef curry
  • Freshly made rice cakes (Pitha)
  • Banana blossom fry
  • Spicy mashed potato with mustard oil
  • Locally grown fruits like pineapple and banana
  • Traditional herbal tea
